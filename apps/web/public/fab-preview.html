Global Leaders Convene Emergency Summit
on Artificial Intelligence Governance

Representatives from over 60 nations gathered in Geneva this week for an emergency summit aimed at establishing international norms around the development and deployment of large-scale AI systems, amid growing concerns about disinformation, autonomous weapons, and economic disruption.

The summit, convened by the United Nations, follows a series of high-profile incidents in which AI-generated content was used to manipulate public opinion ahead of elections in multiple countries. Delegates are expected to adopt a non-binding framework by Friday.

Critics argue that any framework lacking enforcement mechanisms will be insufficient, while industry groups have warned against regulations that could stifle innovation and cede ground to authoritarian states with fewer restrictions.
